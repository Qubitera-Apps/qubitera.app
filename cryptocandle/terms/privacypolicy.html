Privacy Policy

The privacy of our users is of importance to us. This privacy policy page outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by CandleStick Patterns App and how it is used.

Information Collection and Use

The CandleStick Patterns App may use tools or third-party analytics software to automatically collect and use non-personal data that does not directly identify you. Non-personal data can be the following or more; your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, pages of our App you visit, the time and date of your visit, time spent on those pages and other statistics, device software platform and firmware, unique device identifier or other device identifier. The CandleStick Patterns App may use collected non-personal data to analyze the use of the Services and to further improve the Services.

Your Notification and Watch list are stored on the Google could services to deliver notifications reliably and with minimum latency. Apple device APN token or Google's Firebase Cloud Messaging token (FCM) is also stored. APN or FCM token is a long string of characters to send notifications to your device. The token is completely anonymous and doesn’t reveal anything about your device or identity.

Third party service providers used by the app;

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • RevenueCat Subscription
  • Google Firestore Database
