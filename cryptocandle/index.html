CryptoCandle: Patterns for Cryptocurrencies

Auto-scans Japanese candlestick patterns on cryptocurrency daily charts and predicts future price behavior for each coin. Recommendations for market action.

Pattern Finder

Analyzes daily cryptocurrency chart data and detects candlestick patterns to help users identify potential market trends. Using pattern recognition, it provides insights into price movements and helps traders make informed decisions.

Detailed View

Offers a detailed graphical view that visually represents the relationship between coins and identified patterns. This helps users better understand market behavior by displaying trends, formations, and key price movements in an intuitive and interactive way.

Trend Predictions

Provides trend forecasts and actionable insights. It helps users understand how to take advantage of these patterns by suggesting optimal buy points, stop loss levels and sell targets. This strategic guidance enables traders to make informed decisions and effectively manage risk in the crypto market.

Historical Stats

Enhances trading analysis by offering historical data to evaluate the success rates of candlestick patterns over time. Since each cryptocurrency reacts differently to these patterns, the app helps users understand coin-specific behaviors and probabilities. This data-driven approach allows traders to refine their strategies based on past performance, improving decision-making and risk management.

Learn Patterns

Provides comprehensive insight into candlestick patterns by providing detailed definitions and structured information. It categorizes patterns based on their characteristics, interpretation, confirmation within market context, and practical usage. This organized approach helps traders understand how each pattern works, when it is most reliable, and how to apply it effectively in their trading strategies.

Get Notifications

Keeps users informed with personalized notifications whenever a tracked coin forms a recognized candlestick pattern. This feature ensures that traders never miss important market signals, allowing them to react quickly to potential opportunities.
